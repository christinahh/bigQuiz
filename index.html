House of Mirrors

You are well aware of the subjective view you have of yourself, and don’t
shy away from trying to understand and confront it. You find value in
seeing your varied reflections and using the information to better
understand the person you are.
This assessment was created with the intention to encourage users to self-reflect and consider the meaning behind their responses to online personality tests. It is meant to encourage critical thought about the results one receives upon completing an online test. Much like this example, they are often self-inventory style questionnaires where input is solely dependent on an individual’s own view of themselves, albeit with the questions pertaining to specific psychological theories. The results typically involve the presentation of a series of traits and behaviours, all organized under either an archetype or percentages on various binary scales.
